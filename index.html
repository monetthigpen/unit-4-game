Crystal Game

  1. You will be given a Random Number at the beginning of the game.
  2. There are four crystanls below. By clicking on each of them you will add a specific amount of points to your Total Score.
  3. You win by matching your Total Score to the Random Number
  4. You lose if your Total Score is higher than the Random Number
  5. The value of the crystal is hidden to you until you click on the crystal.
  6. Each time the game starts the values of the crystal will change.
